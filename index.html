RupeeRisk
India Macro & Geopolitical Forex Intelligence

Engineered 15 geopolitical tension indicators across 4 transmission channels — Oil Supply, Direct FX, Risk-Off, Trade Route — using STL decomposition and 1-week exogenous lags to eliminate look-ahead bias. SARIMA/ARIMAX/Lasso/Gradient Boosting ensemble with 52-week rolling backtest. Lasso model outperformed SARIMA baseline by 17.3pp directional accuracy and generated a backtested trading strategy with positive Sharpe and cumulative return.

01 — Who I am

About me

I'm a data scientist with a Statistics Honours foundation and a deep curiosity about how numbers explain the world — whether it's a rupee moving on a geopolitical event, India's defence supply-chain exposed to a single supplier, or a student's screen habits quietly affecting their grades.

My work spans fintech, strategic intelligence, public health, and logistics — not because I couldn't pick a lane, but because I believe the most interesting insights live at intersections. Every project I've built starts with a genuine question, not a tech stack.

Before data science, I cleared the AFCAT and CDS written examinations and progressed to SSB selection for the Indian Air Force. That process taught me more about structured thinking under pressure, long-term discipline, and operating in ambiguous environments than any classroom assignment has since.

I'm currently open to Data Analyst, Risk Analyst, Quant Analyst, and Data Scientist roles — especially in fintech, financial analytics, and research-driven teams.
