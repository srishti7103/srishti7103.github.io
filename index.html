01 — Who I am

About me

I'm a data scientist with a Statistics Honours foundation and a genuine curiosity for how numbers explain the world — whether that's a rupee moving on a geopolitical event, India's defence supply-chain exposed to a single nation, or a student's screen habits quietly shaping their grades.

My work spans fintech, strategic intelligence, NLP, public health, and logistics. Not because I can't pick a lane, but because I believe the most interesting insights live at intersections. Every project I've built starts with a real question, not a tech stack.

Before data science, I cleared the AFCAT and CDS written examinations and progressed to SSB selection for the Indian Air Force. That process taught me more about structured thinking under pressure, operating in ambiguity, and long-term discipline than any classroom assignment.
